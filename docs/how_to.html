Overview#

The how-to guides offer a more comprehensive overview of all the tools ðŸ¤— Datasets offers and how to use them. This will help you tackle messier real-world datasets where you may need to manipulate the dataset structure or content to get it ready for training.

The guides assume you are familiar and comfortable with the ðŸ¤— Datasets basics. We recommend newer users check out our tutorials first.

[!TIP] Interested in learning more? Take a look at Chapter 5 of the Hugging Face course!

The guides are organized into six sections:

  • General usage: Functions for general dataset loading and processing. The functions shown in this section are applicable across all dataset modalities.

  • Audio: How to load, process, and share audio datasets.

  • Vision: How to load, process, and share image and video datasets.

  • Text: How to load, process, and share text datasets.

  • Tabular: How to load, process, and share tabular datasets.

  • Dataset repository: How to share and upload a dataset to the Hub.
